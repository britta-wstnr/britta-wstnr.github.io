Student assistant for teaching

What?#

We are looking for a student assistant for teaching in the Bachelor Biomedical Sciences (Biomedische Wetenschappen).

Boris Konrad and Britta Westner are looking for a student to support the teaching of workgroups and practicals. The teaching covers topics such as:

  • How can electrical signals of the body, for example of muscles, be recorded and analyzed?
  • How can pandemics be modelled (using MATLAB and Simulink)?
  • What the effect of x-rays on the human body?

All the teaching is very hands-on and you will help the students with questions that arise or problems they run into. If you want a more detailed look at how and what we teach, then you can download an example of a practical here (pdf).

While the material is mostly in English, the help you will provide can also be in Dutch.

When?#

We are looking for a student in the period of September 2025 – April 2026. The teaching follows an irregular schedule, some weeks are busy while others are free. The teaching schedule will be discussed with you based on your availability.

Who?#

We are looking for students with a background in science (Natuurkunde or Natuurwetenschappen) or other students with a talent for Physics or natural sciences.

How?#

If you are interested, contact either Boris or Britta. We are looking forward to hearing from you!
